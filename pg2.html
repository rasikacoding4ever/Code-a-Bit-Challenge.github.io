Crime Rates Decline

Overview

California is one of states with the highest rates of crime rates all year around. Data proves that crimes have declined during the pandemic in notorious towns within California. Data was recorded before COVID-19 was declared a pandemic and also during the pandemic. Dramatic decrease of the occurence of crimes can be seen in the numbers of both the chart and graph, therefore indicating that crime rates have subsided due to the pandemic.
Note: Numbers on the chart aren't exact since they weren't provided on the website. They are estimated to be as close as possible with the graph as reference.

Property Crimes Decline in California Due to COVID
Town Before Pandemic (Jan-Feb 2020) During Pandemic (Mar 2020-current)
Los Angeles 1,900 1,400
