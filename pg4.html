Reduction in Traffic Accidents

Overview

Spain is one of the many countries with extremely high traffic accident rates. Data provides that traffic accidents have reduced during the pandemic. Data was recorded before COVID-19 was declared a pandemic and also during the pandemic. The chart and graph below show change in weekly traffic accidents before and during COVID lockdown in 2019 and 2020. The data belongs to Taragonna, Spain. The graph has been split in two parts: before and after lockdown. The bars shaded in grey indicate how the 2019 before and during lockdown accident rates were. Similiarly, the bars shaded in black indicate how the 2020 before and during lockdown accident rates were. The reduced height of the bars in the "during lockdown" side is prominent compared to the "before lockdown" side. Therefore, the pandemic has improved our lives by reducing traffic accidents.
Note: Numbers on the chart aren't exact since they weren't provided on the website. They are estimated to be as close as possible with the graph as reference.
