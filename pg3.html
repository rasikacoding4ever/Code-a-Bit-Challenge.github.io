Environmental Benefits

Overview

NO2, also known as Nitrogen Dioxide, is a very harmful emission to the environment caused by fossil fuels combustion which is released by factories and vehicles. The numbers in the chart represent the concentration of NO2 in its respective city. The data was collected once before COVID-19 was declared a pandemic (Feb 2020) and once during lockdown (Apr 2020). Dramatic decrease can be seen in the numbers, therefore indicating that nature healed from the pandemic. Below the chart is a graph that displays the same data.

Improved Air Quality within 3 Months of the Pandemic Across the Globe: NO2 Concentration
City Before Pandemic (Feb 2020) During Pandemic (Apr 2020)
Madrid, Spain 30 9.3
New York City, USA 23 9.1
Delhi, India 40 8.0
Rome, Italy 31 7.0
