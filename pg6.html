Anxiety and Depression

Overview

Anxiety and Depression has been around for a long time. It can be caused by loneliness or failure in life. Anxiety and Depression can lead to serious decisions such as drug abuse and sucide. Thanks to the pandemic, apart from killing more than 4,000,000 people and creating havoc in world economy, the virus has been taking a heavy toll on mental health as well. Below in the table and graph, data displayed shows how the percentage of adults with anxiety and depression significantly increases as the pandemic prolongs. Both the chart and graph display the same data. Who knows how much higher those bars on the graph will rise?

Pandemic Causes Spike in Anxiety & Depression
Symptom 2019 May 2020 December 2020
Anxiety Disorders 8.2% 28.2% 36.9%
Depressive Disorders 6.6% 24.4% 30.2%
Anxiety or Depressive Disorders 11% 33.9% 42.4%
