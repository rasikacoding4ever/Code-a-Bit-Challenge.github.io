Education

Overview

Education is key to future success and a better world for the growing generation. Unfortunately, this might not be met if so many as 1.38 billion students around the world are unable to switch to remote learning due to Covid. Few reasons might be poverty or unavailability of technology, but the bigger reason is the pandemic. With parents losing jobs, they are unable to support their children's education. This results in large numbers of students who are unable to learn online. Below is the chart and graph displaying the number of students unable to learn virtually. Significant increase can be seen in the number of students as the pandemic goes into March. It truly is devestating when we look around and understand how many people are suffering in several ways due to the Coronavirus.
